Our Services

At Junior's Custom Towing LLC, we are more than just a towing company. We are a family. For over two decades, we have proudly served the state of Arizona, providing reliable, efficient, and professional towing services that our customers can trust. Our story began with a single truck and a determination to deliver exceptional service to our community. As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of trust, reliability, and respect. We are proud to have built a reputation that reflects these values. Over the years, we have grown significantly, but our commitment to our customers has remained unwavering. Today, we have a full fleet of tow trucks and a dedicated team ready to respond to any roadside emergency. Our extensive experience in the industry, combined with our state-of-the-art equipment, allows us to offer a range of services to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Through hard work and dedication, we have transformed our humble beginning into a robust operation. But at our core, we're still the family business that treats every customer like they're our neighbor. Our journey of over two decades has been filled with rewarding experiences and valuable lessons.
